The Recipe
  • Soak Brown Rice in Apple Cider Vinegar for 2 hours before cooking.
    • Rinse Well!
  • Add Sweet Potatoes, Yams or Pumpkin.
    • Add any variety of Vegetable EXCEPT Corn and Potatoes.
  • Add variety of Fruits (Blueberry, Apple, Bananas).
  • Cook everything very well, almost to the point of mush.
  • You are done!
    • Product stays fresh up to 3 days in refridgerator or 3 months in freezer.
